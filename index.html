Disease Level Select A Disease

A comparison of the prevalence of diseases within the population of the selected individual with that of the total population is presented in the chart below. Hover on the bars to see the prevalence, or click on the bars to find out more information about the disease.

Mutation Level Select A Mutation

From left to right, the tree below provides additional detail on genes and mutations related to your selected disease. If there are no additional nodes on the tree, there are not any known genes/mutations associated with that disease. Click on the mutation to find out more information.



About Making Genomics Accessible

Genomics is a burgeoning field in genetics allowing researchers to analyze the function and structure of the genome. The field is highly technical and often inaccessible to the non-PhDs of the world. We aim to improve the accessibility of genomics through an easy-to-use visualization.
